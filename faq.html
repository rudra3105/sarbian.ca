Frequently asked questions

  • 01. What is Sasrbian College, and how does it operate?

    Sarbian College is an online institution offering high-quality education. Our programs are fully online, allowing students to learn from anywhere at their own pace.

  • 02. Are there any in-person classes or physical campuses?

    No, Sarbian College is entirely online. All courses, lectures, and assignments are conducted through our virtual learning platform.

  • 03. What programs does Sarbian College offer?

    We offer a range of IT-related courses, including Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Software Development, Cybersecurity, and Data Science.

  • 04. How do I apply for a program at Sarbian College?

    You can apply online via our admissions page by filling out the application form and submitting the necessary documents. Our admissions team will assist you throughout the process.

  • 05. Is Sarbian College accredited?

    Yes, Sarbian College is fully accredited, ensuring our programs meet international educational standards.

  • 06. How are exams conducted at Sarbian College?

    Exams and assessments are completed online through our secure platform. Instructions are provided to guide students through each test.

  • 07. How do I know if you’ve received my application?

    Sarbian College provides 24/7 technical support, access to academic advisors, and online resources like virtual libraries to support your learning journey.
