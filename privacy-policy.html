Privacy policy

  • Home
  • Privacy policy

At Sarbian College, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect:

We may collect personal information from you in various ways, including but not limited to:
When you enroll in our courses
When you fill out forms on our website
When you communicate with us via email or other channels
The types of information we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, payment details, and any other data necessary for providing educational services.

2. How We Use Your Information:

We use the information collected to:
Provide you with the services you’ve requested, such as course enrollment and access to educational materials
Improve our website and service offerings
Communicate important updates, including course information, changes to policies, and promotional offers
Ensure a secure, efficient user experience by preventing fraud and maintaining data integrity

3. Data Protection and Security:

We take the security of your data seriously and implement various measures to safeguard your information. We use encryption protocols, secure servers, and regularly updated systems to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal data.

4. Sharing of Information:

Sarbian College does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you. These parties are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information and use it only as necessary to provide the services we’ve engaged them for.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

Our website may use cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us improve the functionality of our site, analyze user behavior, and remember your preferences. You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use some features of our site.

6. Your Rights:

You have the right to:
Access the personal information we hold about you
Request corrections to any inaccuracies in your information
Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal or contractual obligations
If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@sarbian.ca.


7. Changes to This Policy:

Sarbian College reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.


8. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at:
Email: contact@sarbian.ca
Phone: +1 (672) 232 0573
Address: Toronto, Canada
