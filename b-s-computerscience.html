Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Course descriptions

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program offers a comprehensive foundation in computing principles, programming languages, algorithms, and software development. Students will gain a deep understanding of both theoretical and practical aspects of computer science, including data structures, operating systems, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. This degree prepares students for a wide range of careers in technology and innovation.

Professional experience

Throughout the program, students will work on real-world case studies and use industry-standard tools such as Python, R, and SQL to analyze and interpret data. Collaborative projects will help students develop their analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Career opportunities

Graduates can pursue various roles, including Software Developer, Systems Analyst, IT Consultant, Data Scientist, and AI Engineer. With computer science driving innovation in every industry, the opportunities are vast for graduates to excel in tech-driven roles.

  • Master popular programming languages such as Python, C++, and Java
  • Gain practical experience in software development, database management, and artificial intelligence
  • Build a strong foundation for a career in technology and innovation

Credit and pathways

This program provides a strong foundation for further studies in computer science, data science, AI, or related fields. Credits may be transferred towards specialized certifications or postgraduate studies in technology and computing fields. Graduates are well-prepared to pursue advanced degrees or certifications in areas like machine learning, cybersecurity, and more.
